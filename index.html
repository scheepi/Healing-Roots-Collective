Meet the Team

Rooted in our diverse lived experiences as women of color, we provide culturally responsive, affirming care that honors identity, nurtures growth, and strengthens the pathways that shape who we are and how we relate to others.

Hi, I'm Ashley—it's wonderful to meet you! Our Services

We offer a range of therapeutic services tailored to meet you and your loved ones wherever you are on your healing journey.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions designed to help you explore your thoughts, feelings, and experiences in a confidential, supportive environment. Ideal for anyone seeking personal growth, coping strategies, or healing from past experiences.

Couples Therapy

Strengthen your relationship through improved communication, deeper understanding, and conflict resolution. We support couples at every stage—whether building a stronger foundation or working through challenges together.

Family Therapy

Work together as a family to improve communication, resolve conflicts, and navigate transitions. Family therapy helps strengthen bonds and create a more supportive, harmonious home environment.

Sex Therapy

A safe, non-judgmental space to address intimacy concerns, sexual health, and relationship dynamics. Our certified sex therapists help individuals and couples explore these sensitive topics with care and professionalism.

Play Therapy

Designed for children ages 3-12, play therapy uses creative expression to help young ones process emotions, work through challenges, and develop healthy coping skills in a fun, child-centered environment.

Psychological Assessment

Comprehensive evaluations to better understand cognitive, emotional, and behavioral functioning. Assessments can clarify diagnoses, inform treatment planning, and provide valuable insights for individuals of all ages.

Group Therapy

Connect with others who share similar experiences in a supportive group setting. Group therapy offers community, shared learning, and the powerful reminder that you are not alone on your journey.

Our Values

Everything we do is guided by these core principles that shape how we care for you.

Holistic Care

We see you as a whole person—mind, body, and spirit. Our approach considers all aspects of your life and well-being.

Person-Centered Approach

You are the expert on your own life. We honor your experiences, respect your pace, and collaborate with you every step of the way.

Comprehensive Support

From individual sessions to family work, we offer a full spectrum of services to support your entire journey toward wellness.

Grounded Presence

Like the roots of a tree, we provide a stable, calming presence where you can feel safe to explore and grow at your own pace.

Evidence-Based Practices

Our therapeutic approaches are grounded in research and proven methods, ensuring you receive effective, high-quality care.
